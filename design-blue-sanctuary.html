Blue Sanctuary

Figma Healthcare UI/UX Web Design

A clean, professional web design for Blue Sanctuary — a medical clinic platform focused on patient experience. The design combines clinical precision with a warm, approachable aesthetic to build trust and guide patients through services, doctors, and appointment booking.

Gallery

Problem

Many medical clinic websites feel either too clinical and cold, or too generic to convey expertise. Blue Sanctuary needed a web presence that communicated both medical authority and genuine patient care — while making appointment booking and service discovery effortless.

Solution

Designed a multi-page website in Figma that balances trust and warmth. A clean white and blue palette conveys professionalism while lifestyle imagery and testimonials humanize the brand. Key pages — Home, Services, Doctors, and Contact — were structured for clear user journeys from awareness to booking.

Key Features

  • Hero section with strong brand statement and primary CTA
  • Specialized care pathways section with department cards
  • Doctor profiles grid with credentials and booking links
  • Clinical departments page with service descriptions
  • Patient testimonial section to build social proof
  • Appointment booking CTA with same-day availability indicator

Design Process

01

Research & Benchmarking

Studied leading healthcare websites to understand best practices in trust-building UI, information hierarchy, and patient-focused navigation patterns.

02

Visual Identity

Established the color system — clean white backgrounds with blue accents — alongside typography choices that balance authority and approachability.

03

Wireframing

Mapped out the full site structure and laid out low-fidelity wireframes for all key pages before moving into high-fidelity design.

04

High-Fidelity Design

Built all pages in Figma with a reusable component system — cards, buttons, navigation, and section layouts — for consistency across the site.

05

Prototype

Linked all pages into a clickable Figma prototype to simulate the full patient browsing experience from landing to appointment booking.
