Blue Sanctuary

Figma Healthcare UI/UX Web Design

A clean, professional web design for Blue Sanctuary — a medical clinic platform focused on patient experience. The design balances clinical precision with a warm, approachable aesthetic to build trust and guide patients through services, doctors, and appointment booking.

Gallery

Overview

The Blue Sanctuary project focused on creating a clean and professional web design for a medical clinic platform. The primary goal was to balance clinical precision with a warm and approachable aesthetic — building patient trust while guiding them through services, doctor profiles, and appointment booking.

  • Project Title: Blue Sanctuary
  • The Challenge: Many medical clinic websites feel either too clinical and cold, or too generic to convey expertise. Blue Sanctuary needed a web presence that communicated medical authority and genuine patient care — while making appointment booking and service discovery effortless.
  • My Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Project Duration: Approximately 3–4 months
  • Tools Used: Figma

Research & Discovery

To build a website that instills trust and improves the patient experience, research was conducted on best practices from leading healthcare websites and the needs of both patients and medical professionals.

  • User Research: Studied leading healthcare websites to understand best practices in trust-building UI, information hierarchy, and patient-focused navigation patterns. Also conducted interviews with patients and medical staff to understand their needs and pain points.
  • User Personas: Developed personas representing different user types — such as the "New Patient" searching for information, and the "Returning Patient" booking a follow-up — to guide design decisions.
  • Competitive Analysis: Benchmarked against leading healthcare websites to identify best practices, available features, and opportunities to differentiate Blue Sanctuary.

Ideation & Strategy

The strategy centered on designing a multi-page website with clear user journeys — from initial information discovery through to appointment booking — providing a seamless and informative experience.

  • User Journey Maps: Mapped out key pages (Home, Services, Doctors, Contact, Appointment Booking) with clear user journeys from awareness to booking — emphasizing simplicity and transparency at every step.
  • Information Architecture (IA): Defined the full site structure with sections for services, doctor profiles, testimonials, and contact information — ensuring easy navigation and access throughout.
  • Wireframes (Low-Fidelity): Laid out low-fidelity wireframes for all key pages before moving into high-fidelity design — enabling early feedback on layout and functionality.

Design & Execution

The Blue Sanctuary design was built to deliver a professional and approachable aesthetic — using a clean color system and a reusable component library for consistency across the site.

  • Design System: Established a clean white and blue color system with typography choices that balance authority and approachability. Used lifestyle imagery and testimonials to humanize the brand. Built a comprehensive component library — cards, buttons, navigation, and section layouts — for site-wide consistency.
  • High-Fidelity Designs: Built all pages in Figma with a reusable component system for consistency across the site — showing the final visual design of every screen with complete detail.
  • Prototypes: Connected all pages into a clickable Figma prototype to simulate the full patient browsing experience from the landing page through to appointment booking.

Testing & Iteration

A clickable prototype was created with a focus on usability testing — ensuring the website effectively met patient needs and resolved any identified friction in navigation or booking.

  • Usability Testing: Conducted usability testing with target users (patients) to identify issues in navigation, finding information, the appointment booking process, and overall user experience.
  • Feedback & Changes: Based on testing feedback, design revisions were made to improve usability, visual appeal, and the overall patient experience — resulting in a more refined and effective healthcare platform.

Results & Reflection

The project resulted in a website that balances trust and warmth — making appointment booking and service discovery easier for patients, and expected to increase engagement and appointment volume.

  • Impact / Metrics: Designed a multi-page website that balances trust and warmth — simplifying appointment booking and service discovery, and expected to increase the number of appointments and improve overall patient experience.
  • Key Takeaways: The project reinforced the importance of building a website that communicates both professionalism and empathy — and its direct impact on establishing trust between a clinic and its patients.
  • Next Steps: Potential additions include online consultation features, a patient portal for medical records, and integration with electronic health record (EHR) systems.
