Bottle

FigmaE-commerceProduct UIUI/UX

A clean and minimal product-focused UI design for Bottle — a modern e-commerce or brand landing experience centered on elegant product presentation, conversion-driven layout, and a refined visual identity.

Gallery

Overview

Bottle is a minimal product UI design concept focusing on clean brand presentation and a seamless shopping experience. The design strips away visual noise to put the product front and center — using generous whitespace, refined typography, and purposeful color accents to guide the user's eye and drive action.

The design philosophy is "less is more" — every element on the screen earns its place through function or brand expression, nothing else.

Key Features

  • Hero section with full-bleed product photography and minimal copy overlay
  • Product detail page with variant selector, size guide, and add-to-cart flow
  • Clean checkout flow optimized for conversion with minimal friction
  • Brand story section with editorial layout and lifestyle imagery
  • Mobile-responsive layout with fluid grid and adaptive typography
  • Subtle micro-interactions and hover states for premium feel

Design Process

01

Brand Positioning

Defined the brand as premium, minimal, and modern. Explored type pairings and color palettes that communicate quality without being loud.

02

Layout Exploration

Sketched and wireframed multiple homepage layouts. Selected the hero-first approach with a strong visual hierarchy leading to the CTA.

03

Typography & Color

Chose a serif display font paired with a clean sans-serif body. Applied a restrained two-tone palette — near-white background with deep charcoal and a single accent color.

04

Component Design

Built all components from scratch in Figma — product cards, navigation, forms, and modals — each following the minimal design language.

05

Prototype & Review

Created a clickable prototype covering the core shopping flow from landing page to order confirmation.
