Damayan

FigmaSocial AppMobileUI/UX

A community-centered mobile app UI designed to connect people in times of need — enabling mutual aid, resource sharing, and community support within local neighborhoods and barangays.

Gallery

Overview

Damayan is a community mutual aid mobile app designed to strengthen the Filipino bayanihan spirit in a digital format. The app connects neighbors, barangay members, and community groups — making it easy to offer help, request assistance, and coordinate local support during everyday challenges or times of crisis.

The design is warm and approachable, using a color palette that feels human and trustworthy rather than clinical or corporate — reflecting the community values at the heart of the product.

Key Features

  • Community feed for posting help requests and offers within your barangay
  • Resource matching — connect people who need something with those who have it
  • Event coordination for community activities and relief operations
  • Verified local organizations and volunteer group profiles
  • In-app messaging for direct coordination between members
  • Emergency alert system for barangay-level notifications

Design Process

01

Research & Empathy Mapping

Studied existing community apps and barangay communication patterns. Mapped user needs around trust, simplicity, and local relevance.

02

Visual Direction

Chose a warm, earthy color palette with green accents to evoke community, growth, and bayanihan. Avoided cold corporate tones.

03

User Flow Design

Mapped the core flows: posting a request, responding to help, joining a community group, and sending an emergency alert.

04

High-Fidelity Screens

Designed all screens in Figma at mobile resolution. Applied consistent spacing, iconography, and illustration style throughout.

05

Prototype

Built an interactive Figma prototype covering the onboarding, feed, and help request flows.
