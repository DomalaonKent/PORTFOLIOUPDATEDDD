Travel App

FigmaMobile AppTravelUI/UX

A travel discovery and trip planning mobile app UI. Designed to inspire wanderlust while providing practical tools for itinerary building, destination browsing, and booking management — all in a vibrant, photo-forward visual style.

Gallery

Overview

The Travel App is a mobile UI concept designed to bridge travel inspiration and practical trip planning. Full-bleed destination photography drives visual engagement, while underlying structures keep the booking and planning experience intuitive and stress-free.

The design language draws from modern travel platforms — airy, full-screen imagery paired with semi-transparent card overlays to maintain readability without losing the scenic backdrop.

Key Features

  • Destination discovery feed with curated collections and trending spots
  • Full-screen destination detail view with photos, highlights, and reviews
  • Trip itinerary builder with drag-and-drop day planning
  • Integrated flight and hotel search with filter options
  • Saved trips and wishlists with shareable links
  • Currency converter and weather widget on destination pages

Design Process

01

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed apps like Airbnb, Booking.com, and Wanderlog. Identified opportunities in itinerary building and discovery feed UX.

02

Visual Direction

Chose a light, airy color palette with teal and coral accents. Prioritized photography as the primary design element across all screens.

03

Screen Architecture

Designed a tab-based navigation system: Explore, Plan, Bookings, Profile. Mapped all user paths within each tab.

04

High-Fidelity Screens

Built 20+ screens covering key flows. Applied consistent spacing, elevation, and motion principles across all components.

05

Micro-interactions

Defined transition animations and micro-interaction specs for the prototype — parallax scrolling on hero images, spring animations on cards.
