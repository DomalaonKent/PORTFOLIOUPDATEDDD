Lipstick

Figma E-Commerce UI/UX Web Design

A sleek, elegant e-commerce UI design for a lipstick brand — crafted to evoke luxury and femininity through rich tones, refined typography, and an intuitive shopping experience.

Gallery

Problem

Many cosmetic brands lack a digital storefront that truly reflects the premium quality of their products. Generic layouts fail to capture the elegance and emotion of a beauty brand, leading to lower engagement and conversions.

Solution

Designed a high-end e-commerce experience centered on visual storytelling. Rich imagery, bold product photography, and a refined color palette work together to elevate the brand and guide customers through a seamless browsing and purchasing flow.

Key Features

  • Hero section with full-bleed product imagery and brand statement
  • Product catalog with shade swatches and hover previews
  • Product detail page with shade selector and description
  • Shopping cart and checkout flow design
  • Lookbook / editorial section for brand storytelling
  • Minimal navigation to keep focus on products

Design Process

01

Brand Mood

Established a luxury beauty aesthetic — deep reds, nudes, and soft neutrals with elegant serif typography to convey sophistication.

02

User Flow

Mapped the customer journey from landing to checkout — prioritizing product discovery, shade selection, and a frictionless add-to-cart experience.

03

High-Fidelity Design

Designed all key screens in Figma with full brand system applied — consistent spacing, typography scale, and component library.

04

Prototype

Built an interactive Figma prototype of the full shopping flow for presentation and usability review.
