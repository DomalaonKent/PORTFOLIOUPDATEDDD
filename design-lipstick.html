Lipstick

Figma E-Commerce UI/UX Web Design

A sleek, elegant e-commerce UI design for a lipstick brand — crafted to evoke luxury and femininity through rich tones, refined typography, and an intuitive shopping experience.

Gallery

Overview

The Lipstick E-Commerce project focused on creating an elegant e-commerce UI design for a lipstick brand. The primary goal was to showcase the premium quality of the product and provide an intuitive shopping experience — enhancing engagement and conversions.

  • Project Title: Lipstick E-Commerce
  • The Challenge: Many cosmetic brands lack a digital storefront that truly showcases the premium quality of their products — resulting in lower engagement and conversions due to generic layouts. A platform was needed that could convey the beauty and emotion of a beauty brand.
  • My Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Project Duration: Approximately 1–2 months
  • Tools Used: Figma

Research & Discovery

To build an e-commerce platform that truly conveys luxury and femininity, understanding the target market and their beauty product shopping preferences was essential.

  • User Research: Conducted market research and target audience analysis to understand lipstick consumer preferences, their online shopping habits, and their expectations from a luxury beauty brand.
  • User Personas: Developed personas representing different types of lipstick consumers — such as "Fashion-Conscious Millennial" and "Luxury Brand Enthusiast" — to guide the design and ensure their needs were met.
  • Competitive Analysis: Reviewed competitor websites in the beauty and cosmetics industry to identify best practices, opportunities for differentiation, and visual trends that fit a luxury brand.

Ideation & Strategy

The strategy focused on creating a seamless customer journey — from product discovery to purchase completion — prioritizing visual storytelling and easy navigation.

  • User Journey Maps: Mapped out the customer journey from the landing page to checkout — prioritizing product discovery, shade selection, and a friction-free add-to-cart experience.
  • Information Architecture (IA): Outlined the website's information structure, including product categories, product detail pages, shopping cart, and checkout flow.
  • Wireframes (Low-Fidelity): Created low-fidelity wireframes for key website pages to quickly visualize layout and information flow, enabling early feedback on layout and functionality.

Design & Execution

The design was built to deliver a high-end experience, using rich imagery and a refined design system with complete visual design and an interactive prototype.

  • Design System: Established a luxury beauty aesthetic with deep reds, nudes, and soft neutrals, and elegant serif typography. Used rich imagery and bold product photography. Built a comprehensive design system with components for buttons, cards, and navigation to ensure consistency.
  • High-Fidelity Designs: Designed all key screens in Figma with the full brand system applied — ensuring consistent spacing, typography scale, and component library.
  • Prototypes: Built an interactive Figma prototype of the full shopping flow for presentation and usability review — allowing stakeholders to experience the website flow before development.

Testing & Iteration

Testing the prototype was essential to ensure the shopping experience was smooth and met user expectations — and to identify any usability issues.

  • Usability Testing: Conducted usability testing with target users to identify any issues with navigation, product discovery, the checkout process, and the overall user experience.
  • Feedback & Changes: Based on feedback from usability testing, design changes were made to improve usability, visual appeal, and the overall user experience — resulting in a more refined and effective e-commerce platform.

Results & Reflection

This project resulted in an e-commerce platform that enhances visual storytelling and provides a seamless purchasing experience — increasing engagement and conversions for the lipstick brand.

  • Impact / Metrics: Designed a high-end e-commerce experience centered on visual storytelling to elevate the brand and guide customers through a seamless browsing and purchasing experience — expected to increase sales and brand loyalty.
  • Key Takeaways: The project emphasized the importance of creating an immersive and visually rich experience in e-commerce for luxury beauty brands, and its impact on driving customer engagement and conversion.
  • Next Steps: Potential addition of features such as virtual try-on, personalized recommendations, or social media integration for broader reach and customer engagement.
