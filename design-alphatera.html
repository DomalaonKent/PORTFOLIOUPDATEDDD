Alphatera Construction Supply

Figma B2B Platform UI/UX Web Design

A bold, industrial-themed UI/UX design for Alphatera Construction Supply — a B2B platform for construction materials. Designed in Figma to communicate strength, reliability, and professionalism through dark tones and structured layouts.

Gallery

Problem

Alphatera had no digital presence — potential B2B clients couldn't find their services online, and the sales process was entirely manual. They needed a platform that could showcase their materials, services, and project portfolio while building credibility with buyers.

Solution

Designed a dark, industrial-themed B2B website that communicates trust and capability. Used bold typography, construction-themed imagery, and an orange/rust accent palette to establish a strong brand identity. The layout prioritizes quick access to services, project showcase, and contact — the key conversion points for B2B clients.

Key Features

  • Hero section with strong brand statement and CTA
  • Services showcase with category cards and descriptions
  • Project portfolio gallery with filter by type
  • Our Team page with role-based staff profiles
  • Contact page with inquiry form and location map
  • Admin dashboard design for backend content management

Design Process

01

Brand Discovery

Defined the visual identity — dark navy and rust/orange palette inspired by construction materials and industrial environments.

02

Information Architecture

Mapped out all pages and user flows: public site (Home, Services, Projects, Team, Contact) and admin dashboard flows.

03

Wireframes

Created low-fi wireframes for all pages to validate layout, content hierarchy, and navigation before applying visual design.

04

High-Fidelity Design

Produced polished screens with the full brand system applied. Used heavy hero images, structured grids, and clear CTAs throughout.
