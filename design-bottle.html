Bottle

FigmaE-commerceUI/UXProduct Design

A minimal e-commerce UI concept for a premium water bottle brand. Focused on clean product presentation, strong visual hierarchy, and a frictionless purchase flow — designed to convert browsers into buyers through elegant simplicity.

Gallery

Problem

Premium product brands often struggle to translate their physical quality into digital experiences. Generic e-commerce templates don't communicate brand value, leading to low perceived quality and poor conversion rates — especially for design-conscious buyers.

Solution

Designed a minimal, editorial-style e-commerce interface that lets the product speak for itself. Large product photography, generous whitespace, and a focused color palette create a premium feel that matches the physical product's quality positioning.

Key Features

  • Full-screen hero product photography with color switcher
  • Minimal product detail page with feature highlights
  • Clean add-to-cart flow with size and variant selection
  • Editorial "About" section with brand story
  • Streamlined checkout page design

Design Process

01

Brand Direction

Established a premium minimal aesthetic — white space, muted earth tones, and clean sans-serif typography to signal quality without excess.

02

Conversion-Focused Layout

Structured the product page around the buyer's decision journey: see the product → understand the value → add to cart — with zero distractions.

03

High-Fidelity Screens

Designed complete screens in Figma including hover states, variant selection, and the full checkout flow.
