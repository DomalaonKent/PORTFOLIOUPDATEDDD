Explore the Philippines

Figma Travel App Mobile UI/UX

A conceptual mobile application designed to promote tourism in the Philippines — giving users a platform to discover destinations, access travel information, and plan their trips with ease. Built to highlight the beauty of the country and inspire local and international travelers.

Gallery

Overview

Explore is a conceptual mobile application designed to promote tourism in the Philippines. The goal was to create a platform where users can discover unique destinations, access comprehensive travel information, and plan their trips — enhancing the overall travel experience for both local and international visitors.

  • Project Title: Explore the Philippines
  • The Challenge: Many potential travelers struggle to discover unique destinations in the Philippines and find comprehensive information in one place. There was a need for a user-friendly platform that showcases the country's beauty and simplifies trip planning.
  • My Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Project Duration: Approximately 2–3 months
  • Tools Used: Figma

Research & Discovery

Research was conducted to understand the needs of travelers and the challenges they face when discovering and planning trips in the Philippines — identifying key users and studying existing solutions to inform effective design decisions.

  • User Research: Conducted interviews and surveys with potential tourists and local travelers to understand their trip planning habits, pain points in finding information, and desired features in a travel app.
  • User Personas: Developed personas representing different traveler types — such as the "Adventure Seeker" and the "Culture Enthusiast" — to guide design decisions and ensure diverse needs were addressed.
  • Competitive Analysis: Reviewed existing travel apps and tourism websites to identify best practices, market gaps, and opportunities to differentiate the Explore app.

Ideation & Strategy

Based on research findings, the strategy and information architecture were defined to create a user-friendly and informative mobile app — including user flow planning and initial layout structures.

  • User Journey Maps: Mapped out user journeys for key tasks such as discovering destinations, viewing place details, reading reviews, and saving favorites — ensuring a smooth and intuitive experience throughout.
  • Information Architecture (IA): Defined the app structure with sections for Destinations, Articles, User Profiles, and Saved Destinations — ensuring easy navigation and access to information.
  • Wireframes (Low-Fidelity): Created low-fi wireframes for all key screens to quickly visualize layout and information flow — enabling early feedback on structure and functionality.

Design & Execution

Ideas and strategy were translated into an actual design — focused on creating a clear, efficient, and visually appealing mobile interface with a consistent visual identity that reflects the beauty of the Philippines.

  • Design System: Built a design system with a warm and inviting color palette inspired by the Philippines' natural landscapes, readable typography, and clear iconography. Ensured visual consistency throughout the entire app.
  • High-Fidelity Designs: Designed all final screens in Figma with complete visual design — including detailed UI elements, destination imagery, and interactive components that showcase the overall look and feel of the app.
  • Prototypes: Built a clickable Figma prototype to simulate the interactive user experience and present the flow to stakeholders for review and usability testing.

Testing & Iteration

To ensure the design's effectiveness, usability testing was conducted and feedback was gathered from users — continuously improving the experience to better meet their needs.

  • Usability Testing: Conducted usability testing with target users to identify issues with navigation, feature comprehension, and the overall experience of discovering destinations and planning trips.
  • Feedback & Changes: Based on testing feedback, design revisions were made to improve usability, visual appeal, and the overall user experience — resulting in a more refined and effective travel app.

Results & Reflection

The Explore app aims to provide an easy-to-use platform for discovering destinations in the Philippines — boosting tourism interest and inspiring travelers to explore the country.

  • Impact / Metrics: The designed mobile app provides a comprehensive platform for destination discovery, reading travel articles, and trip planning — expected to increase interest in Philippine tourism and improve the overall traveler experience.
  • Key Takeaways: The project reinforced the importance of creating a visually engaging and informative platform for tourism — and its direct impact on inspiring travelers to explore unique destinations.
  • Next Steps: Potential additions include booking integration (flights and hotels), personalized recommendations, and social media integration for broader reach and user engagement.
