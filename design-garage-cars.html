Garage Cars

A conceptual web design for an automotive brand focused on showcasing vehicles, energy information, and charging options. Designed with a clean, modern interface that highlights key vehicle features and encourages inquiries from potential buyers.

Overview

Garage Cars is a conceptual web design project for an automotive brand aimed at showcasing vehicles, energy information, and charging solutions. The primary goal was to deliver a clean, modern interface that communicates a sense of performance and efficiency — making it easy for potential buyers to explore vehicle features and initiate inquiries.

  • Project Title: Garage Cars
  • The Challenge: Automotive websites are often overly complex or lacking in information — making it difficult for potential buyers to quickly understand key vehicle features and request a quote. The design needed to communicate modernity and efficiency while keeping the experience simple and direct.
  • My Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Project Duration: Approximately 1–2 months
  • Tools Used: Figma

Research & Discovery

Research was conducted on best practices from leading automotive websites and the needs of potential car buyers — to ensure the design meets their expectations and decision-making process.

  • User Research: Conducted market research and target audience analysis to understand car buyer preferences, online research habits, and expectations from an automotive website.
  • User Personas: Developed personas representing different buyer types — such as the "Tech-Savvy Car Enthusiast" and the "Practical Family Buyer" — to guide design decisions and ensure their needs were addressed.
  • Competitive Analysis: Reviewed competitor websites in the automotive industry to identify best practices, available features, and opportunities to differentiate Garage Cars.

Ideation & Strategy

The strategy focused on designing a clean, easy-to-use website that highlights key vehicle information and encourages inquiries — with a clear information structure and user flow.

  • User Journey Maps: Mapped the user journey from the landing page through to requesting a quote — prioritizing vehicle discovery, feature comprehension, and a frictionless inquiry process.
  • Information Architecture (IA): Defined the site structure with sections for Vehicles, Energy, Charging, and Discover — ensuring easy navigation and access to information.
  • Wireframes (Low-Fidelity): Created low-fi wireframes for all key pages to quickly visualize layout and information flow, enabling early feedback on structure and functionality.

Design & Execution

The Garage Cars design was built to deliver a modern experience with a clean aesthetic and clear information hierarchy — complete with full visual design and an interactive prototype.

  • Design System: Established a clean, modern aesthetic with white and grey backgrounds and yellow accents for calls-to-action. Used large typography and high-quality vehicle imagery. Built a comprehensive design system with components for navigation, cards, and buttons for visual consistency.
  • High-Fidelity Designs: Designed all final screens in Figma with complete visual design — including detailed UI elements, imagery, and interactive components that reflect the overall look and feel of the website.
  • Prototypes: Built a clickable Figma prototype to simulate the user experience and present the flow to stakeholders for usability testing and review.

Testing & Iteration

Testing and iteration were conducted to ensure the design effectively met user needs and resolved any usability issues in navigation and the inquiry process.

  • Usability Testing: Conducted usability testing with target users to identify issues in navigation, finding information, the inquiry process, and overall user experience.
  • Feedback & Changes: Based on testing feedback, design revisions were made to improve usability, visual appeal, and the overall experience — resulting in a more refined and effective automotive website.

Results & Reflection

The project resulted in a website that delivers a clean, modern experience — making vehicle discovery and inquiry effortless, and expected to increase user engagement and leads.

  • Impact / Metrics: The designed website provides a clean and modern browsing experience that simplifies vehicle discovery and the inquiry process — expected to increase user engagement and drive more customer inquiries.
  • Key Takeaways: The project reinforced the importance of creating a visually compelling and informative website for automotive brands — and its direct impact on driving customer interest and inquiry conversions.
  • Next Steps: Potential additions include a virtual test drive feature, a car configurator tool, and social media integration for broader reach and customer engagement.
