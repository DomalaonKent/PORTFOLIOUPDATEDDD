Barbershop App

FigmaMobile AppUI/UXBooking

A mobile app UI designed for a local barbershop — streamlining appointment booking, showcasing barber portfolios, and reducing walk-in wait times. Built to improve the overall customer experience and operational efficiency for small barbershop businesses.

Gallery

Overview

The Barbershop App is a mobile UI project designed to streamline appointment booking for a local barbershop. The primary goal was to reduce walk-in wait times, showcase barber portfolios, and improve the overall customer and business experience through a clean, fast, and intuitive mobile interface.

  • Project Title: Barbershop App
  • The Challenge: Local barbershops were losing customers due to unmanaged long queues and a lack of online visibility. Customers had no way to book appointments in advance, check barber availability, or view portfolios — leading to frustration and no-shows.
  • My Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Project Duration: Approximately 1–2 months
  • Tools Used: Figma

Research & Discovery

User research was conducted to identify the target user base of the barbershop app — essential for building a solution that effectively addresses their needs and preferences.

  • User Research: Conducted interviews and surveys with potential barbershop customers and barbers to understand booking habits, pain points with existing systems, and desired features in a booking app.
  • User Personas: Developed personas representing different customer types — such as the "Busy Young Professional" looking for quick booking, and the "Loyal Customer" who wants to see their preferred barber — to guide design decisions.
  • Competitive Analysis: Reviewed existing barbershop booking apps and general appointment scheduling platforms to identify best practices, available features, and opportunities for differentiation.

Ideation & Strategy

The strategy centered on designing a simple and fast booking journey to improve the customer experience — with a clear information structure and user flow.

  • User Journey Maps: Mapped out the booking journey: browse barbers/services → select a barber → choose a service → pick a time slot → confirm appointment. Steps were minimized to reduce drop-off and speed up the process.
  • Information Architecture (IA): Defined the app structure with sections for Home, Barber Profiles, Services, Booking Calendar, and Appointment History — ensuring easy navigation and access.
  • Wireframes (Low-Fidelity): Created low-fi wireframes for all key screens to quickly visualize layout and information flow, enabling early feedback on structure and functionality.

Design & Execution

The barbershop app was designed with a clean, masculine aesthetic optimized for mobile — delivering a simple and fast experience with a complete visual design and interactive prototype.

  • Design System: Clean, masculine aesthetic focused on simplicity and speed. All screens were designed in Figma with large tap targets, bottom navigation, and thumb-friendly layouts. Defined color palette (dark tones with accent colors), typography, and iconography for visual consistency.
  • High-Fidelity Designs: Designed a mobile-first booking app that allows customers to browse barbers, choose a service, select a time slot, and confirm appointments — showing the final appearance of all screens with complete visual design.
  • Prototypes: Built an interactive Figma prototype of the full booking flow for usability testing and stakeholder review — allowing users to experience the app flow before development.

Testing & Iteration

An interactive prototype was built with a focus on usability testing to ensure the app effectively met user needs and resolved identified pain points.

  • Usability Testing: Conducted usability testing with target users to identify any issues with navigation, feature comprehension, and overall booking experience.
  • Feedback & Changes: Based on testing feedback, design revisions were made to improve usability, visual appeal, and the overall booking experience — resulting in a more refined and effective app.

Results & Reflection

The barbershop app aims to streamline the booking process and improve the customer experience by providing easy access to barber services — increasing customer satisfaction and business loyalty.

  • Impact / Metrics: The app simplifies appointment booking, showcases barber portfolios, and reduces walk-in wait times — expected to increase booking numbers and improve customer retention.
  • Key Takeaways: The project reinforced the importance of creating a fast, frictionless booking experience for service-based businesses and its direct impact on customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
  • Next Steps: Potential additions include in-app payments, loyalty programs, and social media integration for broader reach and customer engagement.
