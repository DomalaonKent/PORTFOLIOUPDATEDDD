Barbershop App

FigmaMobile AppUI/UXBooking

A mobile app UI for a local barbershop — designed to streamline appointment booking, showcase barber portfolios, and reduce walk-in wait times. Clean, masculine aesthetic with a focus on simplicity and speed.

Gallery

Problem

Local barbershops lose customers to long unmanaged queues and lack of online visibility. Customers have no way to book ahead, see barber availability, or check portfolio styles — leading to frustration and no-shows.

Solution

Designed a mobile-first booking app that lets customers browse barbers, select a service, pick a time slot, and confirm appointments in under a minute. The barber profile pages show past work portfolios to build trust before booking.

Key Features

  • Home screen with featured barbers and quick book CTA
  • Barber profile pages with portfolio gallery
  • Service selection with duration and price display
  • Time slot picker with real-time availability
  • Booking confirmation and appointment history
  • Push notification design for reminders

Design Process

01

User Persona

Defined target users: busy young professionals who want quick, hassle-free bookings without phone calls or waiting in line.

02

User Flow

Mapped the booking journey: browse → select barber → choose service → pick time → confirm. Minimized steps to reduce drop-off.

03

Mobile UI Design

Designed all screens in Figma optimized for mobile — large tap targets, bottom navigation, and thumb-friendly layouts.

04

Prototype

Created an interactive Figma prototype of the full booking flow for usability testing and stakeholder review.
